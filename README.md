# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence solution built using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze employee data and identify workforce trends.

This dashboard helps HR teams monitor **employee attrition, workforce demographics, job ratings, and employee distribution** to support better decision-making and improve employee retention strategies.

---

## 🎯 Key Metrics Displayed

The dashboard highlights important HR KPIs such as:

* **Total Employees:** 467
* **Attrition Count:** 31
* **Attrition Rate:** 18.24%
* **Active Employees:** 139
* **Average Employee Age:** 32

These KPIs provide a quick overview of workforce status.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 1️⃣ Department-wise Attrition

* Visualizes attrition distribution across departments:

  * R&D
  * Sales
  * HR
* Helps identify departments with higher turnover rates.

### 2️⃣ Employees by Age Group

* Displays employee distribution across age groups:

  * Under 25
  * 25–34
  * 35–44
  * 45–54
  * Over 55
* Provides insights into workforce demographics.

### 3️⃣ Job Role Rating Analysis

* Evaluates employee performance ratings for different job roles such as:

  * Healthcare Representative
  * Laboratory Technician
  * Manager
  * Manufacturing Director
  * Research Scientist
  * Sales Executive
  * Sales Representative

### 4️⃣ Education Field Attrition

* Shows attrition percentage by education background:

  * Human Resources
  * Technical Degree
  * Marketing
  * Other

### 5️⃣ Attrition by Gender & Age Group

* Analyzes attrition trends between **male and female employees** across age groups:

  * 25–35
  * 35–55
  * Over 55
