🛒 Super Store Sales Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize Super Store sales performance across different regions, categories, segments, and shipping modes. This dashboard helps uncover business insights related to sales, profit, customer behavior, and product performance through dynamic and visually appealing reports.

🚀 Features
📊 Sales Analysis by Category & Sub-Category
🌍 Region-wise Sales Performance
📈 Monthly Sales & Profit Trends
🚚 Shipping Mode Analysis
💳 Payment Mode Distribution
👥 Customer Segment Insights
🗺️ State-wise Sales & Profit Visualization
📌 KPI Cards for:
Total Sales
Total Orders
Total Profit
Average Delivery Time
🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
Power BI
Excel
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Business Intelligence & Data Visualization

📈 Key Insights
Technology category generated the highest sales among all categories.
Standard Class shipping mode was the most frequently used delivery method.
The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
Western region recorded the highest sales performance.
Monthly sales and profit trends helped identify seasonal business patterns.
